# UAE Cabinet Decision No. 111/2022: Regulation of Virtual Assets and Their Service Providers

**Purpose**: Federal-level regulation establishing nationwide VA framework and SCA authority
**Key Assumptions**: Applies UAE-wide; coordinates with local authorities like VARA
**Usage Patterns**: Federal AML/CFT requirements; cross-emirate compliance standards

---

## Overview

- **Issued**: December 12, 2022 (18 Jumada Al-Awwal 1444 H)
- **Effective**: January 13, 2023
- **Authority**: Council of Ministers, UAE
- **Source**: https://uaelegislation.gov.ae/en/legislations/1623

---

## Article 1 - Definitions

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **UAE** | The United Arab Emirates |
| **SCA** | Securities and Commodities Authority |
| **CBUAE** | Central Bank of the UAE |
| **Local Licensing Authorities** | Local authority competent to regulate virtual assets in respective emirate |
| **Free Zones** | Any free zone established inside the UAE |
| **Virtual Assets (VAs)** | Digital representation of value that can be digitally traded, transferred, or used for payment/investment |
| **Virtual Asset Service Provider (VASP)** | Any person who conducts VA activities for or on behalf of another |

---

## Article 2 - Scope of Application

The Decision applies to:
1. All virtual asset activities in the UAE
2. VASPs operating within the UAE
3. Free zone entities conducting VA activities
4. Cross-border VA transactions involving UAE

Exclusions:
- Central bank digital currencies (CBDCs)
- Traditional securities already regulated
- Closed-loop virtual currencies

---

## Article 3 - SCA Authority

The Securities and Commodities Authority shall:
1. **Regulate and supervise** VASPs at federal level
2. **License and register** VASPs where no local authority exists
3. **Coordinate** with CBUAE on payment-related VA services
4. **Cooperate** with local licensing authorities
5. **Implement** AML/CFT requirements per Federal Decree-Law No. 20/2018

---

## Article 4 - Regulated Activities

### Primary VA Activities:
1. Exchange between VAs and fiat currencies
2. Exchange between one or more forms of VAs
3. Transfer of VAs
4. Safekeeping and/or administration of VAs
5. Participation in and provision of financial services related to VA issuance

### Additional Regulated Activities:
6. VA custody services
7. VA advisory services
8. VA portfolio management
9. VA trading platform operations

---

## Article 5 - Licensing Requirements

### Application Requirements:
- Completed application form
- Business plan and operational procedures
- AML/CFT policies and procedures
- Fit and proper documentation for controllers
- Capital adequacy evidence
- Technology and cybersecurity assessments

### Licensing Conditions:
- Minimum capital requirements (as specified by SCA)
- Local presence requirements
- Ongoing reporting obligations
- Periodic audits and assessments

---

## Article 6 - AML/CFT Obligations

VASPs must comply with:
1. **Customer Due Diligence (CDD)**: Verify customer identity before establishing relationship
2. **Enhanced Due Diligence (EDD)**: For high-risk customers and transactions
3. **Transaction Monitoring**: Ongoing surveillance for suspicious activity
4. **Travel Rule**: Information sharing for VA transfers
5. **Record Keeping**: Minimum 5-year retention
6. **Suspicious Transaction Reporting**: Report to Financial Intelligence Unit
7. **Sanctions Screening**: Check against UAE and international sanctions lists

---

## Article 7 - Coordination with Local Authorities

| Emirate | Local Authority | Coordination Mechanism |
|---------|-----------------|------------------------|
| Dubai | VARA | MoU and information sharing |
| Abu Dhabi | ADGM FSRA | Regulatory cooperation |
| Other Emirates | SCA direct supervision | Central regulation |

---

## Article 8 - Consumer Protection

VASPs must:
1. Provide clear risk disclosures
2. Segregate customer assets
3. Maintain adequate insurance coverage
4. Establish complaint handling procedures
5. Protect customer data per Federal Law No. 45/2021

---

## Article 9 - Penalties and Enforcement

### Administrative Penalties:
- Written warnings
- Fines (amounts specified by SCA)
- Suspension of license
- Revocation of license

### Criminal Referral:
- For AML/CFT violations
- For fraud or market manipulation
- For operating without license
