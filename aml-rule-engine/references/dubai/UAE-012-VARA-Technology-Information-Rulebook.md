# VARA Technology and Information Rulebook

**Source**: Virtual Assets Regulatory Authority (rulebooks.vara.ae)
**Effective**: 19 June 2025
**Version**: Updated 19 May 2025

---

## Introduction

This Rulebook sets out the technology governance, cybersecurity, and information management requirements for Virtual Asset Service Providers (VASPs) licensed by VARA in Dubai.

---

## Part I: Technology Governance, Controls and Security

### A. Technology Governance and Risk Assessment Framework

VASPs must establish:

1. **Technology Governance Framework**
   - Board-approved technology strategy
   - Clear roles and responsibilities
   - Technology risk management policy
   - Regular technology risk assessments

2. **Risk Assessment Requirements**
   - Annual technology risk assessment
   - Third-party technology risk assessment
   - Cloud service provider due diligence
   - Vendor management framework

### B. Cybersecurity Policy

VASPs must implement:

1. **Cybersecurity Framework**
   - Information security policy
   - Access control management
   - Network security controls
   - Endpoint protection
   - Security monitoring and logging

2. **Security Controls**
   - Multi-factor authentication (MFA)
   - Encryption at rest and in transit
   - Intrusion detection/prevention systems
   - Security information and event management (SIEM)
   - Regular vulnerability assessments

3. **Incident Response**
   - Incident response plan
   - Incident classification framework
   - Escalation procedures
   - Communication protocols
   - Post-incident review process

### C. Cybersecurity - Other Legal and Regulatory Obligations

- Compliance with UAE Cybercrime Law
- Data protection requirements
- Critical infrastructure protection (where applicable)
- Regulatory reporting obligations

### D. Cryptographic Keys and VA Wallets Management

1. **Key Management**
   - Secure key generation
   - Key storage (HSM requirements)
   - Key backup and recovery
   - Key rotation policies
   - Key destruction procedures

2. **Wallet Management**
   - Hot/cold wallet segregation
   - Multi-signature requirements
   - Wallet access controls
   - Transaction signing procedures
   - Wallet audit trails

3. **Custody Requirements**
   - Minimum cold storage thresholds
   - Insurance requirements
   - Segregation of client assets
   - Proof of reserves

### E. Testing and Audit

1. **Security Testing**
   - Penetration testing (annual minimum)
   - Vulnerability assessments (quarterly)
   - Code security reviews
   - Smart contract audits (where applicable)

2. **Audit Requirements**
   - Independent technology audit
   - SOC 2 Type II (recommended)
   - Cybersecurity audit
   - Compliance audit

### F. Virtual Asset Transactions

1. **Transaction Processing**
   - Transaction validation controls
   - Double-spend prevention
   - Transaction monitoring
   - Anomaly detection

2. **Transaction Security**
   - Secure transaction signing
   - Transaction limits and thresholds
   - Approval workflows
   - Audit trails

### G. Algorithm Governance

For VASPs using algorithmic trading or automated systems:

1. **Algorithm Controls**
   - Algorithm testing and validation
   - Change management procedures
   - Kill switch mechanisms
   - Performance monitoring

2. **Risk Controls**
   - Pre-trade risk checks
   - Position limits
   - Circuit breakers
   - Error handling

### H. Business Continuity, Cybersecurity Events and Risk

1. **Business Continuity**
   - Business continuity plan (BCP)
   - Disaster recovery plan (DRP)
   - Recovery time objectives (RTO)
   - Recovery point objectives (RPO)
   - Regular BCP/DRP testing

2. **Cybersecurity Event Management**
   - Event detection and monitoring
   - Incident classification
   - Regulatory notification (within 24 hours for material incidents)
   - Customer notification requirements

---

## Part II: Information Management

### A. Data Governance

1. **Data Management Framework**
   - Data classification policy
   - Data retention policy
   - Data quality management
   - Data lineage tracking

2. **Data Protection**
   - Personal data protection
   - Cross-border data transfer controls
   - Data subject rights management
   - Privacy impact assessments

### B. Record Keeping

1. **Required Records**
   - Transaction records (7 years minimum)
   - Customer records
   - Audit logs
   - Compliance records

2. **Record Integrity**
   - Tamper-evident storage
   - Regular integrity checks
   - Secure backup procedures

---

## Compliance Timeline

| Requirement | Deadline |
|-------------|----------|
| Technology governance framework | Upon licensing |
| Cybersecurity policy | Upon licensing |
| Penetration testing | Within 6 months of licensing |
| SOC 2 audit | Within 12 months of licensing |
| Full compliance | Ongoing |

---

## Penalties

Non-compliance may result in:
- Regulatory warnings
- Financial penalties
- License conditions
- License suspension or revocation
