# FATF Targeted Update on Virtual Assets and VASPs - June 2025

**Source**: FATF (fatf-gafi.org)
**Published**: 26 June 2025
**Type**: Implementation Review Report

---

## Overview

The Financial Action Task Force (FATF) released its sixth targeted review of global implementation of AML standards for virtual assets (VAs) and virtual asset service providers (VASPs).

---

## Key Findings

### 1. Overall Compliance Status (Recommendation 15)

| Status | Percentage | Change from 2024 |
|--------|------------|------------------|
| Largely Compliant (LC) | 29% | +4% |
| Partially Compliant (PC) | 49% | - |
| Non-Compliant (NC) | 21% | -4% |
| Fully Compliant | 1 jurisdiction | - |

### 2. Risk Assessment Progress

- **76%** of jurisdictions (124 of 163) have conducted ML/TF risk assessments for VAs/VASPs (up from 71% in 2024)
- Only **40 of 138** assessed jurisdictions adequately met risk assessment requirements

### 3. Regulatory Approaches

| Approach | Percentage | Change from 2024 |
|----------|------------|------------------|
| Determined regulatory approach | 82% | +9% |
| Permit VA/VASP operations | 62% | - |
| Prohibition (full or partial) | 20% | +6% |
| Partial prohibition only | 48% of prohibiting | - |

### 4. VASP Licensing/Registration

- **96 jurisdictions** require VASP licensing/registration
- **76 jurisdictions** have actually licensed/registered VASPs in practice (up from 69 in 2024)
- **37%** of jurisdictions apply licensing requirements to offshore VASPs

---

## Travel Rule Implementation

### Global Progress

| Metric | Status |
|--------|--------|
| Jurisdictions with Travel Rule legislation | 58% |
| Jurisdictions enforcing Travel Rule | 42% |
| VASPs with Travel Rule solutions | 65% (estimated) |

### Key Challenges

1. Lack of interoperable technical solutions
2. Sunrise issue (asymmetric implementation across jurisdictions)
3. Unhosted wallet transactions
4. Cross-border enforcement gaps

---

## Emerging Threats Identified

1. **DeFi Platforms**: Increased use for money laundering
2. **Privacy Coins**: Continued AML/CFT risks
3. **Cross-chain Bridges**: Exploited for obfuscation
4. **NFT Markets**: Emerging ML/TF vulnerabilities
5. **Stablecoins**: Rapid growth requiring enhanced oversight

---

## FATF Recommendations

### For Jurisdictions

1. Complete implementation of R.15 and R.16 (Travel Rule)
2. Enhance supervision and enforcement capabilities
3. Conduct regular risk assessments
4. Strengthen international cooperation

### For VASPs

1. Implement robust AML/CFT programs
2. Deploy Travel Rule compliance solutions
3. Enhance transaction monitoring for VA-specific risks
4. Report suspicious transactions promptly

---

- Singapore
- Hong Kong SAR
- United Arab Emirates
- Japan
- South Korea
- Germany

## Next Steps

- Updated guidance expected for DeFi and unhosted wallets
- Enhanced focus on Travel Rule enforcement
- Continued mutual evaluation assessments
