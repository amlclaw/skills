# Hong Kong Stablecoins Ordinance 2025

**Purpose**: Regulatory framework for fiat-referenced stablecoin issuers in Hong Kong
**Key Assumptions**: Applies to stablecoins referencing fiat currencies; administered by HKMA
**Usage Patterns**: Stablecoin issuer licensing; reserve requirements; AML/CFT compliance

---

## Overview

- **Legislation**: Stablecoins Ordinance
- **Passed**: 21 May 2025 (Legislative Council)
- **Effective Date**: 1 August 2025
- **Authority**: Hong Kong Monetary Authority (HKMA)
- **Gazette**: Government Gazette, 29/22/1

---

## Part 1 - Scope and Definitions

### Specified Stablecoin Definition

Stablecoins maintaining stable value "with reference to one or more fiat currencies, or units of account, or stores of economic value"

### Licensing Scope

Mandatory licensing required for:
1. Issuing stablecoins in Hong Kong
2. Issuing stablecoins referencing Hong Kong dollar (anywhere in world)
3. Offering stablecoins to Hong Kong residents

---

## Part 2 - Licensing Requirements

### Minimum Financial Resources

| Requirement | Amount |
|-------------|--------|
| **Paid-up Share Capital** | HK$25,000,000 |
| **Liquid Capital** | HK$3,000,000 |
| **Operating Expense Buffer** | 12 months of operating expenses (excess liquid capital) |

### Governance Requirements

- At least one-third of board must be independent directors
- Fit and proper requirements for controllers
- Hong Kong presence requirements
- Senior management accountability

### Permitted Offerors

Stablecoins may only be offered through five categories:
1. Licensed banks
2. SFC-licensed virtual asset trading platforms
3. Stored value facility (SVF) licensees
4. Licensed stablecoin issuers
5. Other prescribed intermediaries

---

## Part 3 - Reserve Asset Requirements

### Coverage Requirements

| Requirement | Specification |
|-------------|---------------|
| **Backing Ratio** | 100% at all times |
| **Asset Quality** | High-quality, highly liquid assets only |
| **Currency Match** | Reserve denominated in same currency as stablecoin |
| **Segregation** | Completely segregated from issuer's other assets |

### Eligible Reserve Assets

- Cash
- Short-term bank deposits
- Government securities (maturity <= 1 year)
- Central bank deposits

### Valuation

- Market value of reserve assets must equal or exceed par value of circulating stablecoins
- HKMA expects overcollateralization in practice

---

## Part 4 - Redemption Requirements

### Mandatory Redemption Rights

| Requirement | Specification |
|-------------|---------------|
| **Redemption Value** | At par value |
| **Redemption Timeline** | Within 1 business day |
| **Fee Restrictions** | No unreasonable fees |
| **Conditions** | No unduly burdensome conditions |

### Insolvency Protections

- Pro rata claim rights against reserve asset pools
- Priority claims for stablecoin holders
- Segregated reserve protection

---

## Part 5 - AML/CFT Requirements

### Customer Due Diligence

| Threshold | Requirements |
|-----------|--------------|
| Transactions >= HK$8,000 | Full CDD required |
| Lower value transactions | Simplified CDD permitted |

### Travel Rule Compliance

Mandatory information sharing for stablecoin transfers:
- Originator information
- Beneficiary information
- Transaction details

### Ongoing Monitoring

- Transaction monitoring systems
- Suspicious activity detection
- Sanctions screening

---

## Part 6 - Reporting and Disclosure

### Financial Distress Reporting

Changed from subjective triggers to mandatory "immediate" reporting when:
1. Licensee unable to meet obligations
2. Insolvency conditions met
3. Payment suspension

### Regular Reporting

- Periodic reserve attestations
- Regulatory returns to HKMA
- Public disclosures on reserve composition

---

## Part 7 - Enforcement and Penalties

### Criminal Offenses

| Offense | Fine | Imprisonment |
|---------|------|--------------|
| Unlicensed issuance | HK$5,000,000 | Up to 7 years |
| Daily continuing offense | HK$100,000 per day | - |

### Administrative Sanctions

- License suspension
- License revocation
- Directions and restrictions
- Public reprimand

### Due Process Enhancements

- Sanction notices must specify effective dates
- Must mention tribunal review rights
- Detail reprimand terms

---

## Part 8 - Stablecoin Review Tribunal

### Jurisdiction

Review of HKMA decisions relating to:
- License applications
- License conditions
- Enforcement actions

### Powers

- Express authority to stay execution of HKMA decisions
- Review pending completion
- Independent adjudication

---

## Part 9 - Transitional Arrangements

### Timeline

| Date | Milestone |
|------|-----------|
| 21 May 2025 | Ordinance passed |
| 1 August 2025 | Ordinance effective |
| 1 November 2025 | Deadline for existing issuers to apply |
| 31 January 2026 | End of provisional approval period |

### Existing Operators

- 3-month window to submit license application
- Provisional operation permitted during assessment
- HKMA may grant extensions in specific cases
