# AML/CFT/CPF Resources

## Overview

This page provides access to key Anti-Money Laundering, Counter-Financing of Terrorism, and Counter-Proliferation Financing resources for Singapore.

---

## 1. Designated Lists and Sanctions

MAS publishes lists of individuals and entities designated under:
- **United Nations Act ("UN Act")**
- **Terrorism (Suppression of Financing) Act ("TSOFA")**

Regulated dealers should subscribe to the MAS mailing list to receive updated lists of designated individuals and entities.

### Inter-Ministry Committee on Terrorist Designation (IMC-TD)

The IMC-TD acts as Singapore's authority relating to the designation of terrorists. This prohibits any person/entity from dealing with or providing any financial assistance to designated subjects, as required under TSOFA.
